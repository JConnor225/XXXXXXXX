The Perfect Smoothie! pt. 2

Now that your ingredients are together, let's blend!

Steps More Info
Step 1 First, add your liquid base and about half of your fruit.
Step 2 Close the lid on your blender, and blend on low speed until the mixture is smooth.
Step 3 Add the rest of your fruit, and any additional mix-ins such as cinnamon, vanilla and/or granola. Blend it.
Step 4 Finally, add your ice and blend until smooth.
