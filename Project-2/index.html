Make the perfect smoothie!

Here are some easy steps to make a perfect smoothie

Where to start

note: this is a guide to materials and ratios when constructing your smoothie of choice.

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups of your liquid base of choice (try coconut milk or flavored yogurt)
  2. 3/4 cups of your fruit(s) of choice (bananas, melons or peaches make a great base)
  3. 2 cups ice (nothing fancy here)
  4. 1 blender of any kind (I like Vitamixes)

    Optional:

    • 1/3 cup granola or flaxseed for texture
    • Dash of cinnamon, cocoa or vanilla powder to taste
